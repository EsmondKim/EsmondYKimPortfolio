About Esmond Y. Kim

Full stack web developer leveraging professional experience gained as a practicing attorney. Currently working on a team of attorneys, communicating, teaching, and achieving clients' goals. Passionate about learning and growing, as demonstrated by enrollment in the University of Minnesota Full Stack Coding Boot Camp, fall 2020 cohort. Maintaining an A+ average learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, API's, jQuery, AJAX, Jest, Node.js, Express, MySQL, Sequelize, and MongoDB.
